What is Tap to Pay?
Tap to Pay is a contactless payment technology that allows you to make secure transactions by simply tapping your phone or card on a payment terminal.
• Fast and secure payments
• No need to insert cards
• Uses NFC technology
• Encrypted transactions
• Works with most modern devices
